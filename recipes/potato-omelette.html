Potato Omelette (Tortilla Española)

Description

Potato omelette, also known as tortilla Española, is a classic Spanish dish made with eggs, potatoes, and onions. It's a versatile and hearty meal that can be enjoyed warm or cold, making it perfect for breakfast, lunch, or even as tapas.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • Olive oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced potatoes and cook until tender, about 10 minutes.
  2. Add the chopped onion and continue to cook until softened.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Drain excess oil from the potatoes and onions and add them to the beaten eggs. Mix gently.
  5. Return the mixture to the skillet and cook on low heat until the bottom is set, about 5 minutes.
  6. Flip the omelette using a plate to cook the other side. Cook for another 5 minutes or until fully set.
  7. Slide the omelette onto a plate, cut into wedges, and serve warm or cold.
