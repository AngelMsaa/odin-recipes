Tuna Melt Sandwich

Description

The tuna melt is a classic, savory sandwich made with tuna salad, melted cheese, and toasted bread. It's a perfect quick and easy lunch that pairs wonderfully with a bowl of soup or a fresh side salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 can tuna, drained
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Butter for spreading
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix together the tuna, mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. On the unbuttered side, spread the tuna mixture and top with cheddar cheese.
  4. Close the sandwich with the other slice of bread, buttered side out.
  5. Heat a skillet over medium heat and grill the sandwich until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  6. Cut in half and serve immediately.
